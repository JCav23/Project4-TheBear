The Original Beef of Chicagoland, simply known as The Beef, was a family business run by Michael "Mikey" Berzatto which has been a staple in the local community for generations. Following the tragic passing of Mikey, The Beef was left to his younger brother Carmy who has rejuvinated the aging Beef into a destination spot.

  • The old Deli has been revamped into a fine dining restaurant.
  • With a foundation in French Cuisine the team have created a brand new menu inspired by the restaurant's Italian-American heritage.
  • Open for dinner service 6 days a week.

Owned and operated by an award wining team of chefs The Bear, is the new face of an iconic restaurant spot that is sure to delight everyone with its exceptional menu and delightful ambience.
